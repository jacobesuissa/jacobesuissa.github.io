About

A little about
the work

Jacobe Suissa is a Copenhagen-based musician, sound designer, and artist. Trained at the Danish National School of Performing Arts, Jacobe works at the intersection of music, theatre, and visual making.

As a musician, Jacobe writes and performs original songs. Atmospheric, carefully constructed, with a strong sense of place and detail. As a sound designer, Jacobe has created sonic environments for theatre stages across Denmark.

Beyond sound, Jacobe draws, sews, crafts and knits. Practices that share the same attention, and exploration of material that runs through all the work.
